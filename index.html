How to Play

Welcome to the Ping Pong Score Keeper!

Here's how to play:

  • Click "+1" or left and right arrows to increase a player's score.
  • Use the dropdowns to set the maximum score and determine the serving player.
  • Toggle the sound effects on/off using the "Sound" dropdown.
  • Modify the player names in the input fields.
  • Click "Reset" to clear scores and settings.
